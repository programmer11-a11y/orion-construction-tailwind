Biography

Sofia Jensen is a creative force in interior design, specializing in blending modern minimalism with warm, inviting spaces. Based in Copenhagen, she has worked on luxury apartments, boutique hotels, and high-end retail environments, each reflecting her eye for detail and mastery of spatial harmony. Sofia’s academic foundation in both fine arts and interior architecture allows her to approach projects from multiple creative angles. With over 9 years of experience, she is recognized for her ability to interpret client visions into bespoke interiors that balance aesthetics and functionality. Her collaborative style fosters strong partnerships with architects, contractors, and artisans, ensuring her designs are flawlessly executed. Sofia is also known for sourcing unique materials and integrating sustainable solutions without compromising visual appeal.

Sofia is readily available for consultations and design presentations through both in-person meetings and digital platforms. She maintains strong client relationships through regular updates and progress check-ins. Her work often draws inspiration from travel, art, and Scandinavian cultural heritage. Beyond her professional life, Sofia is passionate about photography, culinary arts, and supporting local craft communities.

Project Milestones

2021 – 2025

Luxury Hospitality Interiors (Oslo, Norway)

Sofia specialized in luxury hotel interiors, merging Scandinavian minimalism with tailored comfort. She collaborated with suppliers to source eco-friendly materials that maintained a premium aesthetic. Her work included bespoke furniture design, lighting concepts, and spatial flow optimization. She presented moodboards and VR walkthroughs to clients for approval. This period reinforced her reputation for designing immersive spaces that balanced style, sustainability, and guest experience seamlessly.

2016 – 2021

Residential & Retail Design (Stockholm, Sweden)

At NordicSpaces Studio, Sofia led interior projects for upscale homes and flagship retail stores. She translated client visions into functional layouts, incorporating brand identity into the design. She coordinated with contractors to ensure precision in material application and finishing details. Her designs prioritized both form and function, earning repeat business. Sofia’s ability to adapt her style to diverse environments made her a sought-after creative professional.

2013 – 2016

Creative Design Foundations (Copenhagen, Denmark)

After graduating from her design program, Sofia joined a boutique firm, contributing to residential and commercial interior projects. She gained experience in furniture planning, color theory, and space optimization. She prepared presentation boards, 3D renderings, and technical drawings for client meetings. Working under senior designers, she refined her attention to detail. This phase laid the groundwork for her innovative approach to interior design later in her career.
